Santorini, Greece:

Start date: 20-8-2023

End date: 30-8-2023

Santorini, known since ancient times as Thira, is one of the most famous islands in the world. The fact that you can sit in front of the caldera, enjoy local dishes, a drink or a coffee while gazing at the remarkable beauty of an active volcano is priceless!

Grand Cayman:

Start date: 13-8-2023

End date: 20-8-2023

One of the top things to do in Grand Cayman is swimming with sea creatures at Stingray City. This shallow, sandy area, about 25 miles offshore, is filled with gentle Atlantic stingrays that you can touch and even kiss.

Costa Rica:

Start date: 28-9-2023

End date: 05-10-2023

Craving a travel adventure focused on nature and wildlife? You can't go wrong with a trip to Costa Rica in May. It's only the very beginning of the rainy season, so while you are likely to have some chance of showers, you'll get much better prices for weather that's almost as nice as it is earlier in the year.

New Orleans, Louisiana:

Start date: 17-8-2023

End date: 3-9-2023

May is one of your last chances to visit the "Big Easy" before the city becomes infamously muggy for the summer, and fortunately, it's also one of the cheapest times to visit New Orleans. Your best bet is to plan your trip to New Orleans during the week, if you're looking to save some money. Expect warm days, breezy nights, and plenty of Creole culture.

Istanbul, Turkey:

Start date: 10-8-2023

End date: 15-8-2023

Even on a short itinerary, tourists can experience many of Istanbul's top-rated tourist attractions in May, as the entry lines should be relatively short. Visit the Aya Sofya, the 1,500-year-old church-turned-mosque, arguably the most recognizable landmark in the city.

Cancun, Mexico:

Start date: 1-9-2023

End date: 25-9-2023

Aside from the occasional shower or two, Cancun has fantastic weather in May. The spring breakers have returned home, and resorts are eager to fill their rooms, dropping their prices to ultra-low rates. It'll be hot, but with Cancun's breezy, uncrowded coastline and breathtakingly clear sea, you won't sweat it.

The Outer Banks:

Start date: 1-10-2023

End date: 10-10-2023

The Outer Banks is an idyllic chain of barrier islands stretching over 170 miles of the Atlantic Coast, and it boasts a slew of fantastic things to do.

Lisbon, Portugal:

Start date: 15-10-2023

End date: 19-10-2023

Looking for a deal on Lisbon? Sneak in a vacation to this beloved European city in May, just before prices jump in June, and you'll find that you can get great bang for your buck.
